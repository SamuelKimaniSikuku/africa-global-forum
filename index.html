About the Forum

A platform built for the African century.

Africa Global Forum is the convening organisation for the world's most ambitious African diaspora — the entrepreneurs, executives, investors, technologists, creatives and policymakers whose work crosses borders and changes outcomes.

We exist because the African story abroad is too often told in fragments. Capital flows are not connected to ideas. Talent is invisible to opportunity. Community is built privately, when it could be built collectively. We are the connective tissue.

From flagship summits to private councils, from job boards to investment circles, the Forum turns a scattered diaspora into a coordinated force.

"
The 21st century will be shaped by Africa — and the diaspora is the bridge that carries it there. This Forum is where that bridge gets built.
Founder's Note Africa Global Forum
The Four Pillars

Four pillars. One purpose.

/ 01

Investment Opportunities

Curated deal flow into African markets — venture, infrastructure, real estate, public equities and private credit. Investor circles, syndicates and direct access to funds and founders building the continent.

Explore investments
/ 02

Jobs & Connections

A talent network of senior African professionals across every sector and continent. Executive search, board placements, fellowships, mentorship pairings, and a job board curated for the global African workforce.

View careers
/ 03

Community Driven

City chapters in 30+ cities worldwide. Member-led salons, cultural programming, family networks, and a private platform where Africans abroad find each other, support each other, and build together.

Find your chapter
/ 04

Everything Else

Diaspora banking and remittance partnerships. Tax, immigration and legal advisories. Education access for next-gen members. Health partnerships. Repatriation support. The infrastructure of an African life lived globally.
